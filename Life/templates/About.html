Activities

Floating repeat:

Acitity that you want to be done in a certain time span, e.g. 3 times a week, or every day. Examples are jogging, swimming... You can select the time span and the warning days. #warining days before last time you did it + time span, the floating repeat will appear under your today header as a reminder. You can start planning your activity!

Repeat:

Activity that has a place in your weekly routine. You can select the days the activity will appear under today. Activity can be marked as done, but will return the next 'active day'. E.g. you want to do something every sunday

Reminder:

Just a simple reminder, can be of any kind, later on possibility to always show reminder, or to let reminder pop up at random days (e.g. motivation, or some psychological conlcusion)

Deadline:

Something that has (or you want) to be done before a certain day. You can give a warning time, #warining_time days before the deadline, the deadline will appear under today.

Quotes

A quote is a motivational short text that appears random on top of the welcome screen. A great motivation to start your day.
